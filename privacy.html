Niracle Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Information We Collect

Niracle collects your email address when you create an account. We use this to authenticate your account and send notifications you have opted into.

How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely for account authentication and optional push notifications about redemption codes and game events. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third Party Services

Niracle uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase — account authentication and data storage
  • OneSignal — push notifications
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google AdMob — advertisements for free users

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely via Supabase. We do not store payment information — all payments are handled by Google Play and RevenueCat.

Notifications

Push notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings or within the app.

Children's Privacy

Niracle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
